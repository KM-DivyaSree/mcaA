Kerala

Kerala, often called "God's Own Country," offers a diverse range of tourist attractions, including beaches, backwaters, hill stations, and wildlife sanctuaries. Popular destinations include Munnar, Kochi, Wayanad, Alappuzha (Alleppey), and Thiruvananthapuram (Trivandrum). The backwaters of Vembanad Lake and Kumarakom are also major draws for tourists.
