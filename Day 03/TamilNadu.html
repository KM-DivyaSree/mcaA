Tamil Nadu

Meenakshi Amman Temple

The Meenakshi Amman Temple, also known as Arulmigu Meenakshi Sundareshwarar 
Temple, is a historic Hindu temple located in Madurai, Tamil Nadu.
 It's dedicated to Goddess Parvati, known as Meenakshi, and her consort, 
Lord Shiva, referred to as Sundareshwarar. The temple is renowned for 
its Dravidian architecture, towering gopurams (gateway towers), and 
intricate sculptures.


Brihadeeswara Temple

The Brihadeeswara Temple in Thanjavur was built by the Chola emperor
 Raja Raja Chola I. Construction began around 1003 CE and the temple 
was consecrated in 1009-1010 CE. It is also known as Rajarajeswaram, 
after the king.
